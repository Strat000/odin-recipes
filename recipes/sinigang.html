How to cook a lovely Sinigang na Baboy (Pork Sinigang) Recipe

List of Ingredients:
  • 1 kg pork belly or ribs, cut into cubes
  • 8 cups water
  • 1 onion, quartered
  • 2 tomatoes, quartered
  • 2 radish, sliced
  • 1 bunch sitaw (string beans), cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 1 bunch kangkong (water spinach), leaves separated
  • 1 eggplant, sliced
  • 2 green chili peppers (optional)
  • 1 packet sinigang mix (or fresh tamarind if available)r
  • Salt and fish sauce (patis) to taste
Instructions:
  1. Boil the pork: In a large pot, bring water to a boil. Add the pork, onion, and tomatoes. Simmer until the pork is tender (about 45 minutes to 1 hour).
  2. Add Vegetables: Add the radish and cook for 5 minutes. Then add the eggplant, sitaw, and green chili peppers. Cook until the vegetables are tender.
  3. Add the Sour Flavor: Stir in the sinigang mix (or fresh tamarind juice). Adjust the sourness to your preference by adding more mix or tamarind.
  4. Season and Serve: Add fish sauce or salt to taste. Lastly, add the kangkong leaves and simmer for another 1-2 minutes.
  5. Serve: Serve hot with steamed rice.
