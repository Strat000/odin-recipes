Chicken Adobo Recipe Quick and Easy

List of Ingredients:
  • 1 kg chicken, cut into serving pieces
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 1 cup water
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 2 tsp peppercorns
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
Instructions:
  1. Marinate the Chicken: In a bowl, combine the chicken, soy sauce, and half of the garlic. Marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Cook the Chicken: Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Sauté the remaining garlic and onion until fragrant. Add the marinated chicken (reserve the marinade) and cook until browned.
  3. Simmer: Pour in the reserved marinade, vinegar, water, bay leaves, peppercorns, and sugar (if using). Do not stir immediately after adding vinegar—let it cook for a few minutes.
  4. Cook Until Tender:Simmer on low heat for 30-40 minutes or until the chicken is fully cooked and tender. Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper.
  5. Serve: Serve hot with steamed rice.
