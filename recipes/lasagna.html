How to Make a Lasagna in 6 Easy Steps

List of Ingredients:
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Fresh basil or parsley (optional)
Instructions:
  1. Cook the Noodles: Boil the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Prepare the Meat Sauce: In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Sauté the onions and garlic until soft. Add the ground beef and cook until browned. Stir in marinara sauce, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. Prepare the Cheese Mixture: In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, half of the Parmesan, and salt. Mix well.
  4. Layer the Lasagna:
    • Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce at the bottom.
    • Layer 3-4 lasagna noodles on top.
    • Spread a layer of the ricotta mixture over the noodles.
    • Sprinkle a layer of mozzarella.
    • Repeat the layers: sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella until all ingredients are used up.
    • Finish with a layer of sauce, mozzarella, and the remaining Parmesan.
  5. Bake: Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  6. Serve: Let the lasagna cool for 10 minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh basil or parsley if desired.
